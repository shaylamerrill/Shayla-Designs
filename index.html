About Me



I'm currently searching for a UX or UI internship in the Boston area for the summer of 2020.

Hey! My name is Shayla,

I'm a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design.

After working as a graphic designer for a few years, I felt like it wasn't enough of a challenge, I was getting bored with print media. I still had my love for design, but I wanted something more complex... So I went back to school for web design. I even started teaching myself how to code, intending to have a hand in web development.

But! I found something else... My passion for the design thinking and problem solving that UX design has to offer. The process of creating end to end UX/UI designs that encompass what the client needs, while also looking good is what I strive to accomplish.

I'm always down to collaborate when I have the time. Reach out and let's chat!

Opus

Opus is a customizable AR environment inside airport at the gates. The sound proof walls allows the business traveler a way to get work done in peace.

The ability to completely customize the environment using the app allows the business traveler to fully relax before their flight departs.

CASE STUDY

Toast Shipping

Toast is a POS system for restaraunts.

After being invited to the Toast warehouse and seeing some hardware shippment issues... I rethink their system in a way that gives both the customer and Toast a piece of mind when it comes to making sure all of the customer's hardware has been shipped and will be ready for the day of install.

CASE STUDY

Recall

Everyone has left something at home at one point or another... Instead of having to wrack your brain for that one item you think you're forgetting, wouldn't it be nice to have a visual reminder of the items you've left behind?

Once setup, Recall is a hands off device that does the thinking for you... Letting you know what items you've left behind before you head out the door.
